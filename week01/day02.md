# Day 02 - AI Changes the Development Workflow
**Date:** 2026-07-01  

## Today's Topic
- AI-assisted Development Workflow
- The Role of AI Agents in Software Development

## Fact
I learned that AI is no longer limited to generating code.
AI agents can support the entire software development lifecycle, including planning, implementation, testing, review, and documentation.

## Insight
The biggest shift is not a specific AI product.
The real transformation is that AI agents are becoming active collaborators throughout the software development lifecycle, while humans focus more on decision-making, architecture, and review.

## Connection to My Experience
In my work, I have focused on helping partners successfully integrate APIs by providing documentation, guidance, and best practices.
As AI agents become part of software development, partner enablement may evolve to support both developers and AI agents.
